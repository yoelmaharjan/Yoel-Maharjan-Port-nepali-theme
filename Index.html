About Me

Hi, I’m Yoel Maharjan, a passionate and driven individual with a deep interest in robotics, aviation, and graphic design. I thrive at the intersection of technology and creativity, constantly pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in automation and aerospace engineering. My journey has been shaped by curiosity, innovation, and a relentless drive to make an impact.

Currently, I’m a Grade 12 student from Nepal majoring in Biology, but my expertise and passion extend far beyond traditional science. I have a profound love for robotics and aviation, which has led me to actively participate in competitions, projects, and workshops that focus on automation and aeronautics.

I serve as the Robotics Captain of Team Arniko, leading a talented team of engineers and innovators in designing, building, and programming robots for various challenges. My role involves strategic planning, hands-on technical development, and mentoring younger students to help them grow in the field of robotics and AI.

Beyond robotics, I work as a writer at Avio Space, where I research and write insightful articles about aviation, aerospace engineering, and cutting-edge technologies in the field. My writing blends technical accuracy with engaging storytelling, making complex topics accessible to a wider audience.
